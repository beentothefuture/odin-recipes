Crockpot Lasagna

Description

This is an easy to follow recipe for making crockpot lasagna. It's a great dish for when you want something warm and comfortable. Serve with a salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 yellow onion - chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • pinch salt & pepper
  • 30 ounce jar tomato sauce
  • 12 ounce box of lasagna noodles
  • 12 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 16 ounces of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with the minced garlic and chopped onion in a skillet over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain. Add tomato sauce, salt and pepper, and the oregano. Stir until well combined and heated.
  2. Stir mozzarella, ricotta, and parmesan together in a large bowl.
  3. Using a spoon, create a layer of meat mixture on the bottom of the slow cooker. Add a double layer of lasagna noodles, breaking as necessary to fit inside the crockpot. Top the noodles with a layer of cheese mixture. Repeat layering the sauce, noodles, and cheese until all of the ingredients are used.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours.
  5. Make yourself a salad and pour a glass of your favorite red wine and enjoy this tasty meal!
