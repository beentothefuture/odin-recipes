Vinegar Taffy

Description

Vinegar Taffy. When I first mention the name to people, I get a negative reaction as people imagine the taste of vinegar in a candy. In truth, it's one of the most delicious candies I've ever had, and most people who get past their first reaction and give it a try agree.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup white distilled vinegar
  • pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except the butter in a pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Boil to hard ball stage (~250 degrees f)
  3. Remove from heat and add butter
  4. Lightly grease a platter
  5. Pour the taffy onto the platter and allow it to cool until you can handle it without burning your hands.
  6. Apply a thin layer of butter to your hands. Begin to pull the taffy until it is light in color and stiff (15-45 minutes).
  7. Roll it into a rope. Use the handle of a butter knife to break it into small 1" pieces. Lightly dust with powdered sugar. Wrap in waxed paper and enjoy!
